Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026 · Effective: 16 July 2026

Plain-English summary: Locate is a travel, navigation, and social community app. Your map and trip data stays on your device. If you join Mapmakers, photos or videos you choose to contribute help build community maps — metadata is stripped first (and faces/plates are blurred when full scrub models are enabled), and you can remove any piece at any time. Your social profile, posts, stories, and messages are stored on our secure servers. We show paid advertisements in the social feed. We never sell your personal data.

1. Who We Are

Locate is developed and operated by Balo Services. Contact: locate@locatecommunity.com

2. Age Requirements

Minimum age: 13 in most countries; 16 in Australia, South Korea, Germany, Austria, France, Belgium, Denmark, Netherlands, Norway, and Finland. We collect date of birth and country at registration.

4. Background Location

Used solely for trail recording when the screen is off. A persistent notification is shown while active. You can decline in-app or revoke in device Settings → Apps → Locate → Permissions.

5. Advertisements

Paid advertisements appear in the social feed, labelled “Sponsored.” We use profile country for targeting only — no behavioural profiling or third-party ad networks.
